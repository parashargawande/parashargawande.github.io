### 5 Inch FPV Drone
This project involves the creation of a 5 inch FPV drone using the SpeedyMario 5DC frame, MicroAir F405FC stack, and INAV for flight control.

#### Components:
- SpeedyMario 5DC Frame
- MicroAir F405FC and Stack
- INAV

This drone is designed for high-speed performance and agility, making it suitable for racing and aerial photography.
